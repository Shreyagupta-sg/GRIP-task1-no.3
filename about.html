About Us

Don't turn away, Give today! Don't turn your back to those in need. Donate to charity. Donate, a child needs your hope.

Charity is the act of giving help to those in need of it. It is a humanitarian act. It involves giving money, goods or time and effort to those who need it. It is done without expecting something in return.Poor, sick or injured people are generally considered the proper people to whom charity should be given.
